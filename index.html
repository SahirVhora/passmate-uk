About PassMate UK

PassMate UK is a free, community-built tool to help you prepare for the UK car theory test. Every question covers a topic from the DVSA's official curriculum and aligns with the Highway Code 2025.

All questions are original — written from knowledge of the Highway Code domains, not copied from any commercial app or website. The app works entirely offline after your first visit.
